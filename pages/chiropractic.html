What is Chiropractic Care?

Chiropractic care is a natural, drug-free approach to healing that focuses on treating and preventing musculoskeletal system disorders, particularly those affecting the spine. Our experienced chiropractors use precise, targeted adjustments to restore proper alignment and function to your body.

Our Approach

At Cornerstone Chiropractic, we take a comprehensive approach to your care:

  • Thorough initial assessment and diagnosis
  • Customized treatment plans
  • Regular progress evaluations
  • Integration with other therapeutic modalities when needed

Common Conditions We Treat

  • Back and neck pain
  • Headaches and migraines
  • Sports injuries
  • Sciatica
  • Joint pain and stiffness
  • Poor posture
