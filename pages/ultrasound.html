What is Ultrasound Therapy?

Ultrasound therapy is a non-invasive treatment that uses sound waves to promote healing and reduce pain. The high-frequency sound waves create deep heat in tissues, increasing blood flow and helping to reduce swelling and inflammation while accelerating the healing process.

Treatment Applications

  • Soft Tissue Injury Treatment
  • Joint Pain Management
  • Inflammation Reduction
  • Scar Tissue Treatment
  • Chronic Pain Management

Conditions We Treat

  • Tendonitis and bursitis
  • Joint inflammation
  • Sports injuries
  • Arthritis pain
  • Post-surgical recovery
