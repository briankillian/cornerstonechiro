Our Story

At Cornerstone Chiropractic, we believe wellness is more than the absence of illness—it's about thriving at every stage of life.

Founded in 1999 by Dr. Brian Killian, our clinic was created with the express of redefining traditional healthcare. Dr. Killian recognized that true wellness required more than occasional treatment. Instead, it demands a collaborative approach rooted in prevention, performance, and ongoing partnership.

From day one, we committed ourselves to delivering care that goes beyond the basics. This led us to pioneer a membership model, ensuring consistent, high-quality care was not a luxury but an accessible part of everyday living. Our innovative approach reflects our dedication to empowering patients to actively invest in their health—not just today but for the long term.

Our Mission
